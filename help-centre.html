How to manage your events & quote requests

All of your events and quote requests can be managed through your event planner account. Whether you need to make changes, block communication with vendors, or remove the event entirely, this will all be done through your control panel.

If the details of your event change or you find that you’ve made a mistake when creating your initial quote request, you should update the details right away. You can only make changes before you book someone. Once you’ve accepted a quote and paid your deposit, the event cannot be edited.
