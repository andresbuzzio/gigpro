Cancellation Policy
Edit

Your cancellation policy settings determine what happens to the deposit and balance payment in the event of an unexpected client cancellation. The policy you choose will be displayed to the client at the time of booking. Read more about cancellation policies.

When a booking occurs on GigSalad, we collect the deposit and the balance in one transaction. If you select a refundable deposit, we will hold the deposit until the end of your cancellation window. The balance will be sent to you 1 business day after the event.

Agreement Expiration
Edit

When will your quotes expire?

When sending a quote, you can choose how long before the quote will expire. Once the quote expires you can no longer be booked with that quote. This helps prevent double bookings and creates a sense of urgency for the event planner. If necessary, you can still go in and cancel the quote before it expires.
