How it works?

Gigpro is the one stop for all your business needs. We offer a full-service booking platform that allows you to accept service requests, send quotes, message back-and-forth to your client, and book all in one place. This makes it easy for you to manage all your business needs & gives your clients an easy way to book you. But don’t take our word for it. See what our clients and vendors have to say.

01. Create a Profile.

Join Gigpro, create a profile. Your profile will be displayed to the event planners visiting Gigpro.

02. Get service requests.

Event planners will request a quote through your profile on Gigpro. You will be notified via email/text. You can then respond by sending a quote using our quotation management system.

03. Get Booked.

If the event planner accepts your quote, you are booked! 

04. Provide your services.

Event planners will request a quote through your profile on Gigpro. You will be notified via email/text. You can then respond by sending a quote using our quotation management system.
