How it works?

Gigpro makes your event-planning simple and stress-free. No more hopping from site to site or store to store to create the perfect event. From Weddings to birthdays, corporate events to candlelight dinners, holiday gatherings to retirement parties and a lot more, we’re here to help you find the perfect vendors to bring your event to success. Search our site and you’ll see the best, client-reviewed entertainers, performers, event planners & event vendors. But don’t take our word for it. See what our clients and vendors have to say.

01. Explore.

Share some details about your event & browse through a variety of vendors in your area. See their profile, photos, videos & verified reviews from real clients. Select your favourites.

02. Request.

Submit free quote requests to as many Vendors as you’d like.

03. Compare & Book.

Vendors will send you a price quote to work out the details, you can also chat with vendors using out inbuilt message system. If everything looks good with the quote you receive, you’re ready to book. if not, jump onto next Vendor.
